About

I came across coding during the first lockdown and quickly fell in love, the problem solving mixed with creativity captured my attention and I was soon hooked. Not long after finding coding I started an IT Apprenticeship with then evolved into a full-time position after my Apprenticeship was complete.

Although I enjoyed my new position and loved learning how to build and repair computes as well as how networks work and how to install different network hardware my brain kept going back to coding.

I decided I didnt want to regret not pursuing my passion so I in a Code Institutes Coding Bootcamp where I have been able to work on and expand my existing coding skills. During the course I have been able to build a range of projects from a choose your own adventure JavaScript game to an online library system to fit for every bookworm and my most current project where I am building an e-commerce website.

I am eager to gain entry into the tech industry into an exciting development team where I can continue to develop my skills and grow as a developer.

Bookmarked

Bookmarked is a digital library for users to upload and organize their books. It was created as a way to keep track of what books I have as I often end up giving books away or lending them to friends or family to read resulting in me forgetting what books I have owned or read. I wanted to be able to create bookshelves so I can organize them, and add comments so if I no longer had the book I can look back and see what I thought of it. Finally I wanted to have a feature that would generate a book for me, because I buy books at a faster rate than I can read them it can become overwhelming trying to chose which one to read next. I wanted to be able to generate a book from some parameters such as from a specific author or bookshelf or just generate a book based on my whole library.

Facility 443

Facility 443 is a fully immersive choose your own adventure horror game. You wake up in a room you don’t recognize daze and confused and its your choices that will determine if you get out alive or not. While you explore the environment you can pick up items that will help you determine what’s going on and help you break out of the facility. Do you focus on getting answers and figuring out what’s going on or do you get out as fast as possible? With danger behind every corner, footsteps chasing you and a menacing voice counting down to an event that you’re not sure you want to stick around to see one wrong decision could end it all.
